Who are we?

Uniting mathematicians, computer scientists and econometricians, UNSW DataSoc seeks to empower students with knowledge of data science, machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Fostering a community learning environment, DataSoc is passionate about providing a platform for members to enhance and develop their technical and analytical skills. Workshops, facilitated both internally by members and externally by DataSoc’s corporate partners, equip members with the knowledge and skills to strive in the workforce.

Passionate about the power of artificial intelligence and machine learning, DataSoc is the epicentre of technological innovation at UNSW. Curious learners and experimentalists, our passion and love of technology drives our purpose.
